Wedding Cakes

Our stunning range of wedding cakes complement every style, from boldly modern and delicately romantic to timelessly traditional. Most cakes come in a choice of colours, while a few are available simply iced for the bride’s florist to dress with fresh flowers. Some offer a choice of different cake types by tier, and all come beautifully and safely packaged.

Freshly made to order, using free-range eggs and no artificial colours, every cake tastes as good as it looks. We have been delivering stylish wedding cakes in perfect condition for 20 years, and guarantee that you’ll have a throughly attentive and reliable service.
